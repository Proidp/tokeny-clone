Transforming capital markets

Retail banking is digital. Thanks to new technology, this industry has undergone significant changes over the past decade and consumers now have instant access to digital banking services, 24/7 and 365 days a year.

Capital markets remain analogue. This industry is full of manual and costly processes, leading to complex and disjointed systems. Through tokenization, we enable institutions to digitize financial assets, such as equities, loans, bonds, funds and other securities, to streamline their administration and provide firms with a digital-first service.

How should I apply?

Have a look at our job page, or send us your CV, cover letter, links to social media, articles, videos and whatever you think will make a difference to recruitment@tokeny.com

How long it takes for the recruitment process?

You’ll firstly have a short interview with our Talent Acquisition Manager, followed by the relevant hiring manager (depending on the position you are applying to), then a last interview with our CEO. The timing depends, but usually won’t take longer than two to four weeks.

Can I apply for more than one job at the same time?

Yes, you can apply for multiple openings.

Do you offer internships?

Yes, we do.

What can I do if there is currently no relevant vacancy?

You can still contact us with your CV which we will keep on file and contact you should there be any suitable opportunities.

We invite you to join us in our next chapter to create a better and brighter financial future for all.
